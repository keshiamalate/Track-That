Help & Support

Frequently asked questions

What is an envelope?
An envelope is a budget category where you set a spending limit for the month. For example, a "Groceries" envelope with a ₱8,000 budget.
How do I add a transaction?
Open an envelope and tap "Add Transaction". Enter the description and amount to record your spending.
What does "Overspent" mean?
Overspent means your spending has exceeded the budget set for that envelope.
Can I delete a transaction?
Yes! Open the envelope and tap the trash icon next to any transaction to delete it.
How do I delete an envelope?
Open the envelope, tap the trash icon in the top right, and confirm deletion.
